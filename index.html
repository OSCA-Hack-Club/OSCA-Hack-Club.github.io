We're at our best when we're making.

Code, build and innovate at OSCA!

Discover the joy of code, together.

Welcome to OSCA Hack Club, a place where students come together to learn, build, and create with technology. Whether you're an experienced coder or just starting out, our club is all about making cool things, meeting like-minded people, and having fun along the way.

What do we do?

  • Work on real projects - websites, games, apps, and more.
  • Learn coding skills through hands-on workshops.
  • Collaborate on open-source and community-driven projects.
  • Join the global Hack Club network of teen hackers.

Why join Hack Club?

  • Learn by making - no boring lectures, just real coding.
  • Build projects you care about and show them off.
  • Access amazing resources like Hack Club Bank, workshops, and grants.
  • Be part of a worldwide community of young makers.

Who can join?

We have a maximum capacity of 20 students per meeting, so we recommend that you sign up to secure your place. OSCA Hack Club is open to all students at Ormiston Sandwell Community Academy, regardless of your experience level or background. If you're curious about technology and want to learn more, we'd love to have you join us!

When do we meet?

Hack Club meets every Wednesday after school in S15. We start at 3:05pm and finish at 4:15 pm. If you're interested in joining us, just come along to our next meeting - we'd love to see you there!
